What can I afford?

This is a difficult question to answer, but generally depends on a few areas:

  • Your deposit size
  • Your income
  • Your credit score

Your deposit size

The larger your deposit, the lower risk you are to someone considering offering you a mortgage. The larger the deposit, the lower the interest rate you'll pay on your mortgage.

The sweet spot for deposit sizes is 15% of the market value of your property. The higher the better, but this tends to be the point where you can pick up a comparatively lower mortgage rate, which will reduce the amount you'll repay each month in interest. Your income

A general rule is that you typically borrow around four and a half times your annual salary.
